Our 10 Promises to You

When you book an appointment with Rae's Anatomy Physiotherapy, you're not just booking a session—you're entering into a partnership for your recovery. Here's what you can expect from us:

1

We Will Listen

Your first appointment will start with us truly listening to your story, concerns, and goals—not rushing to treatment.

2

Clear Explanation

We'll explain what's happening in plain English, without medical jargon, so you understand your condition fully.

3

Honest Assessment

If we can't help you, we'll tell you upfront and refer you to someone who can. No unnecessary appointments.

4

Personalised Plan

Your treatment will be tailored specifically to your needs, lifestyle, and goals—not a one-size-fits-all approach.

5

Evidence-Based Care

All our treatments are backed by the latest research and proven clinical evidence—no fads or unproven methods.

6

Realistic Timeline

We'll give you an honest timeframe for recovery based on your condition and our experience—no false promises.

7

Professional Service

You'll always be treated by fully qualified, HCPC-registered physiotherapists with extensive experience.

8

Ongoing Support

Between appointments, we're available for questions and support—you're not left wondering what to do.

9

Respect Your Time

We run on time, respect your schedule, and won't keep you waiting unnecessarily. Your time is valuable.

10

Long-Term Results

We focus on lasting solutions and preventing recurrence, not just temporary relief that fades in days.

💯 Our Money-Back Guarantee

If after your first appointment you don't feel we've listened to you, provided clear explanations, or given you a solid plan forward, we'll refund your appointment fee—no questions asked.

That's how confident we are in the quality of care we provide.
