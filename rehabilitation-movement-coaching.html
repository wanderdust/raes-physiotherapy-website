Rehabilitation & Movement Coaching

1:1 guided support to help you move better, feel stronger, and stay injury-free

What is Rehabilitation & Movement Coaching?

This is a 1:1 coaching service designed to bridge the gap between physiotherapy and real-world movement. It's ideal for those who are no longer in acute pain but aren't yet back to full confidence, strength, or performance.

Working closely with Kirstie, our Movement & Rehab Coach, you'll develop the strength, control, and confidence needed to move well in everyday life, work, or sport.

This Service Is Ideal If You Are

  • Recovering from injury and want to progress safely
  • Finishing physiotherapy but not yet fully confident in your body
  • Looking for expert guidance to improve movement, strength, and technique
  • Wanting consistent 1:1 coaching support to stay accountable and keep progressing

How We Can Help

Each session is tailored to you and may include strength training for your lifestyle or sport, mobility and movement quality, biomechanics and technique coaching, body awareness and neuromuscular control, and building confidence in movement again.

  • A personalised strength and mobility programme
  • Ongoing coaching, progression, and accountability
  • Movement technique feedback and refinement
  • Support to build confidence and independence
  • Improved strength, mobility, and movement quality
  • Increased confidence in your body
