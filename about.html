About Our Expert Team

Our Story

At Rae's Anatomy Physiotherapy, we are proud to be one of the leading physiotherapy clinics in Fife. All our physiotherapists are fully qualified, experienced and CSP/HCPC registered in musculoskeletal and sports physiotherapy. We provide expert assessment and treatment for back pain, sport injuries, joint problems and exercise rehabilitation. We take a unique, whole-person approach considering not just your symptoms, but how your lifestyle, career, and sport affect your recovery. With diverse experience across different domains of physiotherapy, sports science, and massage therapy, we are confident that we can help you get back to doing the things you love.

Rae's Anatomy Physiotherapy is based in Burntisland and Glenrothes, Fife. The clinic was established in May 2023 by Marcus Rae. The clinic is fortunate to work alongside highly skilled podiatrists, massage therapists and reflexologists. With local travel links by train, bus and designated parking area the clinics are easily accessible to those of Fife and surrounding areas.

Marcus Rae

About Marcus

Marcus is the founder and lead physiotherapist at Rae's Anatomy Physiotherapy. With a passion for helping people achieve their health and fitness goals, Marcus combines clinical expertise with a genuine commitment to patient care.

Marcus established the clinic in May 2023 with a vision to provide exceptional physiotherapy services throughout Fife. His approach focuses on evidence-based treatment, clear communication, and empowering patients to take control of their recovery.

As a registered member of the HCPC and CSP, Marcus maintains the highest professional standards. He has specialised training in musculoskeletal physiotherapy, sports injury management, and exercise rehabilitation, making him well-equipped to treat a wide range of conditions.

Marcus Croy

Remedial & Swedish Massage Therapist

Certified Therapist

About Marcus

Marcus is an experienced remedial and Swedish massage therapist who brings a wealth of knowledge and skill to the team. His expertise in soft tissue therapy makes him an invaluable part of the clinic's multidisciplinary approach to treatment.

With specialised training in remedial and Swedish massage therapy, Marcus provides therapeutic massage for those dealing with chronic pain and musculoskeletal conditions. His treatments focus on relaxation, tension relief, and promoting natural healing.

Marcus's approach combines traditional massage techniques with modern understanding of anatomy and biomechanics. He works closely with the physiotherapy team to ensure patients receive comprehensive, coordinated care.

Specialisations
  • Remedial & Therapeutic Massage
  • Swedish Massage
  • Soft Tissue Injury Treatment
  • Myofascial Release Techniques

Fiona Ritchie

MSc Physiotherapist

22+ Years Experience

About Fiona

Fiona is a highly experienced physiotherapist with over 22 years of clinical practice. She holds an MSc in Physiotherapy and has developed extensive expertise across multiple specialisations throughout her distinguished career.

Having worked with NHS Fife for many years, Fiona has treated a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions and developed particular expertise in women's health physiotherapy. She is a recognised specialist in ante-natal and post-natal care, helping new mothers through pregnancy and recovery.

Fiona's patient-centred approach combines evidence-based treatment with compassionate care. She takes time to understand each patient's unique circumstances and goals, developing personalised treatment plans that deliver lasting results.

The Rae's Anatomy Difference

We understand that finding the right physiotherapist can feel overwhelming. Perhaps you've tried other treatments that didn't work, or you're worried about wasting time and money on another disappointing experience. These concerns are completely valid, and we hear them every day.

What Makes Us Different?

✓

We Listen First: Before any treatment begins, we take time to truly understand your pain, your goals, and your concerns. Your story matters.

✓

Evidence-Based Treatment: We don't follow trends or fads. Every technique we use is backed by research and proven to work.

✓

Clear Communication: No medical jargon or confusing explanations. We'll help you understand exactly what's happening and why.

✓

Realistic Expectations: We'll tell you honestly what we can achieve together and how long it might take. No false promises.

✓

Long-Term Results: We don't just treat symptoms. We find the root cause and give you the tools to prevent problems returning.

Your time is valuable, and so is your trust. That's why we're committed to being transparent, honest, and focused on getting you results. If we don't think we can help you, we'll tell you and point you in the right direction.
