About Rae's Anatomy Physiotherapy

Feel better. Move better. Live better.

Our Story

At Rae's Anatomy Physiotherapy, we are proud to be one of the leading physiotherapy clinics in Fife. All our physiotherapists are fully qualified, experienced and CSP/HCPC registered in musculoskeletal and sports physiotherapy. We provide expert assessment and treatment for back pain, sport injuries, joint problems and exercise rehabilitation. We take a unique, whole-person approach considering not just your symptoms, but how your lifestyle, career, and sport affect your recovery. With diverse experience across different domains of physiotherapy, sports science, and massage therapy, we are confident that we can help you get back to doing the things you love.

Rae's Anatomy Physiotherapy is based in Burntisland and Glenrothes, Fife. The clinic was established in May 2023 by Marcus Rae. The clinic is fortunate to work alongside highly skilled podiatrists, massage therapists and reflexologists. With local travel links by train, bus and designated parking area the clinics are easily accessible to those of Fife and surrounding areas.

The Rae's Anatomy Difference

We understand that finding the right physiotherapist can feel overwhelming. Here's what sets us apart.

Before any treatment begins, we take time to truly understand your pain, your goals, and your concerns. Your story matters.

Evidence-Based Treatment

We don't follow trends or fads. Every technique we use is backed by research and proven to work.

Clear Communication

No medical jargon or confusing explanations. We'll help you understand exactly what's happening and why.
