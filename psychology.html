Clinical Methodology

Therapeutic Approach

Evidence-based, collaborative therapy tailored to individual needs

Starting therapy can feel like a significant step. My priority is to create a comfortable, supportive environment from the outset. I work collaboratively, exploring challenges together at a pace that feels appropriate for you.

I utilise Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), an evidence-based approach examining the interconnection between thoughts, feelings, and behaviours. I also integrate third-wave approaches including mindfulness-based strategies, compassion-focused therapy (CFT), and acceptance and commitment therapy (ACT).

Therapy should provide a safe, non-judgemental space for open dialogue, confidence building, and developing effective coping strategies.
