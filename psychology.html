About Sara Rae - CBT Therapist in Fife

Clinical Associate in Applied Psychology • NHS & Private Practice • 9+ Years Experience

Hello, I'm Sara Rae, a Clinical Associate in Applied Psychology, specialising in CBT. Working across NHS and private practice, including addiction support and life coaching, I bring a multicultural perspective shaped by my Guatemalan-Scottish background.

My own experience of adapting to new challenges deeply influences how I support others in their therapeutic journey.

Therapy Services

Anxiety & Stress

CBT-based treatment for generalised anxiety, panic disorder, social anxiety, and stress management using evidence-based techniques.

Depression

Compassionate support and practical strategies for managing low mood, loss of motivation, and depressive episodes.

OCD & Intrusive Thoughts

Specialist CBT and ERP techniques to help you regain control over obsessive thoughts and compulsive behaviours.

Trauma & PTSD

Trauma-informed therapeutic approaches including trauma-focused CBT to support recovery and healing.

Addiction Support

Non-judgmental support for substance use and behavioural addictions, drawing on years of specialist NHS experience.

Chronic Pain

Psychological approaches to help manage the emotional and mental health aspects of living with chronic pain conditions.
