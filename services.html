Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy and wellness services tailored to your individual needs

Expert Care Modern Equipment Personalised Treatment
1

Back and Neck Physiotherapy

Neck and back pain is some of the most common conditions we see but most cases aren't caused by a serious problem. We offer a comprehensive approach to assessment and treatment of neck and back pain, utilising a variety of manual therapy techniques, exercise therapy, and educational advice to help reduce pain and discomfort. Our goal is to help you get back to your normal activity level as quickly and safely as possible.

Learn More
2

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is a form of treatment that involves stimulating specific points on the body with thin needles to help reduce pain and inflammation, by stimulating the bodies natural pain relieving chemicals. It can also promote tissue healing, relaxation and calming effect helping to alleviate tension and improve sleep.

Learn More
3

Sport Injury Rehabilitation

Sports Injury Physiotherapy is a service dedicated to helping athletes and active individuals return to their sport following an injury. Our expert physiotherapists can assess and diagnose your injury, provide treatment and rehabilitation tailored to your sport and goals, and offer advice on sport-specific exercises to help you stay injury-free in the future. With our help, you'll be back to your sport in no time.

Learn More
4

Vestibular Rehabilitation

Vestibular rehabilitation is a specialised form of physical therapy designed to alleviate symptoms associated with vestibular disorders, which affect the inner ear and its communication with the brain. People with vestibular disorders may suffer from symptoms such as vertigo (a spinning sensation), dizziness, visual disturbances, and imbalance. The rehabilitation process typically involves exercises and activities that target specific components of the vestibular system, as well as other sensory and motor systems involved in balance.

Learn More
5

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Physiotherapy should commence soon after surgery to maximise recovery and reduce any secondary complications or restrictions in mobility or function. Whether you have elected for surgery for a total knee/ hip replacement or due to trauma or injuries such as ACL-repair, meniscal repair or shoulder rotator cuff repair. Utilising a firm knowledge of post-operative protocols and rehabilitation exercises physiotherapy can help enable you to get back to the things that matters most to you.

Learn More
6

Remedial Massage Therapy

At our physiotherapy clinic, we offer professional massage services that can help to reduce pain and improve mobility. Our experienced practitioners use a range of techniques to target specific areas of discomfort to provide relief and promote healing.

Learn More
7

Taping / Sports Taping

Various taping and strapping techniques can be used to provide support and protection to injured areas whilst healing and to prevent future injury. Taping and strapping is used widely in sport but can be used to treat painful conditions such as planter fasciitis, ankle sprain and knees injuries to name a few.

Learn More
8

Home / Community Physiotherapy

If your unable to attend the clinic, no problem we can arrange to come to you. Community physiotherapy can be offered to people who are likely to benefit most from treatment in their own environment. Those who are housebound, have a long-term condition or were just discharged from hospital are examples of where this may be more appropriate than seeing them in a formal setting.

Learn More
9

Strength & Conditioning

Strength and conditioning is often thought to be only for athletes, however with the right support and education anyone can benefit from it. There are many benefits to strength and conditioning including, improved bone density, reduce risk of future injury, improves mood and improve sport related performance. Methods include movement control, weight training, plyometrics, speed and agility, mobility/ stability, endurance, and lots more depending on the individual or team's needs.

Learn More
