Services Available at Glenrothes

Our Glenrothes clinic at Balbirnie Fitness Centre offers a comprehensive range of physiotherapy and rehabilitation services.

Back & Neck Physiotherapy

Specialist assessment and treatment for back pain, neck pain, and spinal conditions using evidence-based techniques.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Comprehensive sports injury assessment and rehabilitation programmes to get you back to peak performance.

Acupuncture

Traditional and Western acupuncture for pain relief, muscle tension, and overall wellbeing.

Remedial Massage Therapy

Therapeutic massage targeting soft tissue injuries, muscle tension, and chronic pain conditions.

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Structured recovery programmes following surgery, helping you regain strength and function safely.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on treatment techniques to restore joint movement, reduce pain, and improve function.

Rehabilitation & Movement Coaching

Tailored rehabilitation and movement coaching programmes to support your long-term recovery and fitness goals.

Strength & Conditioning

Personalised strength and conditioning programmes supporting injury prevention and athletic performance.

Sports Taping

Professional sports taping and strapping to support injured joints and muscles during recovery and activity.
